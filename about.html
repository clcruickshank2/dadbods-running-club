About Us

The guys behind the dad bods

Chris

Chris is a Canadian import who landed in Denver two years ago. He's married, has a one-year-old, and a Sheepadoodle named Sammy who requires more attention than most people. A proudly mediocre runner since middle school, Chris is training for two half marathons in 2026 and fully embracing the "Dad Bod" lifestyle.

Jackson

Jackson has been in Denver for four years after relocating from San Diego. Soccer is his true passion, but running comes with the territory — so here we are. He's married with two kids and can often be found out on family walks around Wash Park. He's training for zero half marathons in 2026, but may consider a late entry just to beat Chris.
